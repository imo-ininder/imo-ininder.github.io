Who Am I

I’m Liu, but you can also call me Imo. Currently, I’m based in Tokyo and work as an senior SRE. I specialize in on-premises Kubernetes, including management, development, and resource optimization. Additionally, I am well-versed in AWS, IaC tools, and various cloud-native ecosystem technologies to create robust and scalable infrastructure.

I am also interested in following new technical trends and exploring how to bring them into real-world applications.

Why I Write This Blog

I write this blog to document what I learn and to share my knowledge. I hope it can help others who are also interested in related topics.
